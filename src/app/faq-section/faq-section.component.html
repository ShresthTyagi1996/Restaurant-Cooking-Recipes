FAQ

How to Submit a Recipe? To submit a recipe, please Sign In or Create Account and fill out the form on our website with the recipe details, including ingredients, instructions, and any photos you'd like to share. We review all submissions before publishing.
How to Create Account? To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner of the page. Fill in the required information, including your email address and password. Once you submit the form, you'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.
What to do when you submit the Recipe form? Once you have uploaded all the required Details for the recipe, our admins will check the recipe and if it is approved, it will be published on the website. You will receive a notification email once your recipe is live.
If you have submiited the form and recipe doesn't show up? Send an email to "support@indianrecipes.com" with the subject "Recipe Submission" and include your name, email address, and a brief description of the recipe you submitted. Our team will look into it and get back to you as soon as possible.
